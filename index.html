Highlights

One standout per discipline
Coral resilience · MBE01

Heat-tolerant corals make the cover of Nature

Delgado's lab found three Caribbean genotypes that survived the 2023–24 bleaching with symbionts intact — and outplanted 1,200 fragments in the Keys.

Delgado et al., Nature (2026)
Hurricane forecasting · ATM02

Forecasting model goes operational at the NHC

Bhatt & Morán's “SHIPS-ML+” cuts 24-hr rapid-intensification false alarms by 31% — now in operational test for the 2026 season.

NOAA JTTI · with NHC & AOML
NSF CAREER · MGS03

NSF CAREER award for slow-slip imaging

Okonkwo wins a 5-year, $785K award to image slow-slip events along the Cascadia margin — plus a K-12 “Listening to the Earth” module.

NSF EAR-2614402
Ocean transport · OCE04

Longest-ever Florida Current record published

Karpov, Olsson & the FC-Array team release a 7-year continuous transport record, revealing variability linked to AMOC.

Karpov et al., L&O (2026)
